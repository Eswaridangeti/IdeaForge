# 🚀 IdeaForge AI
### AI-Powered Startup Idea Validation & Decision Intelligence Platform

IdeaForge AI helps entrepreneurs, students, innovators, and startups validate their business ideas using Artificial Intelligence. Instead of spending weeks on market research, users receive instant insights, feasibility analysis, competitor research, financial projections, and actionable recommendations.

🌐 **Live Demo:** https://ideaforgeaitakeoverhackathon.netlify.app/

📂 **GitHub Repository:** https://github.com/Eswaridangeti/IdeaForge.git

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Many entrepreneurs struggle to determine whether their business ideas are practical, profitable, and competitive. Traditional validation requires expensive consultants, lengthy market research, and business expertise. IdeaForge AI simplifies this process by providing AI-powered business validation and decision intelligence in minutes.

---

## 💡 Solution

IdeaForge AI analyzes business ideas using AI to generate:
- Business Viability Score
- Market Analysis
- Competitor Research
- SWOT Analysis
- Financial Projections
- Risk Assessment
- Personalized Recommendations
